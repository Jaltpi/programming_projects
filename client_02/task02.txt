## Ticket Name ##

- Needle in a Haystack

## Ticket Component ##

- LVL_2

##  Background Context  ##

- Our client has recently booked a doctor's appointment for the new year in the month of January,
    but she can't remember where she stored the information for her appointment in her computer.

## Ticket Description ##

- Find the appointment information for our client programmatically to avoid them having to search each folder
   for her file.

## Assumptions/Dependencies ##
- Client provides file structure of her computer and name of her appointment_information.
- Developer understands the following topics:
    - Flow Control
    - Loops
    - Data Structures:
        - Lists
        - Tuples
    - Functions
    - Testing

## Acceptance Criteria ##

- All test cases are passing:
    - Client can find their file regardless of where it is placed in her current directory.
